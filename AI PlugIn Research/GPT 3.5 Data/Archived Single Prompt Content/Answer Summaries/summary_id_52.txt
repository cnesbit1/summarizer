The main characters in the Star Wars saga vary across different trilogies and story arcs within the franchise.
Here are some of the key characters who have played central roles in various Star Wars films and series: Original Trilogy (Episodes IV-VI): Luke Skywalker: The protagonist of the original trilogy, Luke is a Jedi Knight who becomes instrumental in the Rebel Alliance's fight against the Galactic Empire.
He is portrayed by Mark Hamill.
Princess Leia Organa: A leader of the Rebel Alliance, Leia is skilled in diplomacy and a key figure in the fight against the Empire.
She is portrayed by Carrie Fisher.
Han Solo: A charismatic smuggler and pilot of the Millennium Falcon, Han Solo becomes a crucial ally to the Rebel cause.
He is portrayed by Harrison Ford.
Darth Vader (Anakin Skywalker): Once a Jedi Knight named Anakin Skywalker who fell to the dark side, Darth Vader is a powerful Sith Lord and central antagonist.
He is portrayed by David Prowse (body) and voiced by James Earl Jones.
Obi-Wan Kenobi: A wise Jedi Master who mentors both Anakin Skywalker and later Luke Skywalker.
He is portrayed by Alec Guinness.
Yoda: A legendary Jedi Master known for his wisdom and mastery of the Force.
He trains Luke Skywalker in the ways of the Jedi.
Yoda is a puppet and voiced by Frank Oz.
Prequel Trilogy (Episodes I-III): Anakin Skywalker: The central character of the prequel trilogy, Anakin is initially a young slave on Tatooine who becomes a Jedi Knight and ultimately falls to the dark side, becoming Darth Vader.
He is portrayed by Hayden Christensen.
Padmé Amidala: Queen and later senator of Naboo, Padmé is a key figure in galactic politics and Anakin Skywalker's love interest.
She is portrayed by Natalie Portman.
Obi-Wan Kenobi: Younger version of Obi-Wan Kenobi, portrayed by Ewan McGregor, who serves as Anakin Skywalker's Jedi mentor and later Luke Skywalker's mentor.
Qui-Gon Jinn: A wise Jedi Master who discovers Anakin Skywalker and believes he is the Chosen One destined to bring balance to the Force.
He is portrayed by Liam Neeson.
Mace Windu: A senior Jedi Master and member of the Jedi Council, portrayed by Samuel L. Jackson.
Sequel Trilogy (Episodes VII-IX): Rey: The central protagonist of the sequel trilogy, Rey is a scavenger who discovers her Force sensitivity and becomes a key figure in the Resistance.
She is portrayed by Daisy Ridley.
Finn: A former stormtrooper who defects from the First Order and joins the Resistance.
He is portrayed by John Boyega.
Kylo Ren (Ben Solo): The main antagonist of the sequel trilogy, Kylo Ren is the son of Han Solo and Leia Organa who is seduced by the dark side of the Force.
He is portrayed by Adam Driver.
Poe Dameron: A skilled pilot and member of the Resistance who plays a key role in their fight against the First Order.
He is portrayed by Oscar Isaac.
Leia Organa: General Leia Organa continues to lead the Resistance against the First Order, portrayed by Carrie Fisher (using footage from previous films after her passing).
Other Significant Characters: Chewbacca: Han Solo's loyal Wookiee co-pilot and friend, portrayed by Peter Mayhew (Episodes IV-VII) and Joonas Suotamo (Episodes VII-IX).
R2-D2 and C-3PO: Droid companions who appear throughout all three trilogies, providing comic relief and integral support to the main characters.
Emperor Palpatine: The dark lord of the Sith who manipulates events across all three trilogies.
He is portrayed by Ian McDiarmid.
These characters, among others, form the core ensemble of the Star Wars saga, each contributing to the epic narrative that spans generations and galaxies in the galaxy far, far away.

I’ve been going through a crisis of faith lately, and I need some answers.

I used to wholeheartedly believe that Moses, Abraham, and Noah were all real as they were recorded in the Bible, but there appears to be little evidence for their existence from the consensus of archaeologists. Sodom and Gamorrah haven’t been verified, either.

If all these Old Testament stories are true, then why is there a lack of evidence of these people and places.

Jesus and Paul seemed to have accepted many of the people and events in the OT as literal and actual truth, so were they wrong? If I’m to remain a Christian, how do I reconcile all of this?
